Tyrant Unleashed Optimizer

Simulate And Optimize Your Deck
Usage Install FAQ

Tyrant Unleashed Optimizer is a command line tool to simulate and optimize your tyrant unleashed deck based on the famous tyrant_optimize from flash Tyrant.

To start the command line interpreter in windows use Start: cmd.exe, navigate to the folder containing tu_optimizer.exe and type one of the commands displayed below.

If you do not like the command line you can also use SimpleTUoptimzeStarter.exe. Click here for more details.

Simulate your deck against another deck

>tu_optimize "Cyrus, Medic, Revolver, Imperial APC" "Malika, Grunt, Blitz Armor" sim 100000
Your Deck: [RWFDFhH3] Cyrus, Medic, Revolver, Imperial APC
Enemy's Deck: [PyA0Fl] Malika, Grunt, Blitz Armor
win%: 59.443 (59443 / 100000)
stall%: 0.014 (14 / 100000)
loss%: 40.543 (40543 / 100000)
  • cards are identified by their name. By definition Cyrus means Cyrus at max Level (Level 4). If you want to use Cyrus Level 1 use Cyrus-1
  • if you have eg. three copies of Revolver use Revolver(3)
  • by default your deck and enemy's deck are played in random order

Simulate your deck ordered

>tu_optimize "Cyrus, Imperial APC, Revolver, Medic" "Malika, Grunt, Blitz Armor" -r sim 100000
Your Deck: [RWFDH3Fh] Cyrus, Imperial APC, Revolver, Medic
Enemy's Deck: [PyA0Fl] Malika, Grunt, Blitz Armor
win%: 86.192 (86192 / 100000)
stall%: 0.088 (88 / 100000)
loss%: 13.72 (13720 / 100000)
  • -r tries to play your card in the correct order still following the "three card hand limit" rule
  • by default normal PVP mode is used. You play your first card in turn 1

Simulate your deck in surge mode

>tu_optimize "Cyrus, Imperial APC, Revolver, Medic" "Malika, Grunt, Blitz Armor" -r -s sim 100000
Your Deck: [RWFDH3Fh] Cyrus, Imperial APC, Revolver, Medic
Enemy's Deck: [PyA0Fl] Malika, Grunt, Blitz Armor
win%: 64.898 (64898 / 100000)
stall%: 0.083 (83 / 100000)
loss%: 35.019 (35019 / 100000)
  • -s means enemy's deck starts in turn 1. You play your first card in turn 2

  • technically climb and reorder are using a hill climbing algorithm to reach the optimized deck. That means the found optimized deck can not be further improved by adding, removing or swapping a single card. It is a local maximum but there could be still better decks if you change multiple cards. However experience from flash tyrant shows that the decks found are really good. So do not worry to much about this.

>tu_optimize
Tyrant Unleashed Optimizer 2.2.0 - Copyright (C) 2014 zachanassian
usage: tu_optimize Your_Deck Enemy_Deck [Flags] [Operations]

Your_Deck:
  the name/hash/cards of a custom deck.

Enemy_Deck:
  1) semicolon separated list of defense decks, syntax:
     deck1[:factor1];deck2[:factor2];...
     where deck is the name/hash/cards of a mission or custom deck, and factor is optional. The default factor is 1.
     example: "94. Heart of Tartarus" is the deck of mission 94. Heart of Tartarus at Level 10.
     example: "eerie-spam:0.2;nbd-spam:0.8" means eerie-spam is the defense deck 20% of the time, while nbd-spam is the defense deck 80% of the time.
  2) a regular expression surrounded by /.
     regular expression will be used to search all custom decks for matching keys.
     example: "/^GT/" will select all custom decks starting with the letters GT.
     
Flags:
  yfort <your_fortress_cards>: your fortress structures. your_fortress_cards: the name/hash/cards of one or two fortress structures.
  efort <enemy_fortress_cards>: enemy fortress structures. enemy_fortress_cards: the name/hash/cards of one or two fortress structures.
  -e : set the battleground effect.
               use "tu_optimize Po Po -e list" to get a list of all available effects.
  -r: the attack deck is played in order instead of randomly (respects the 3 cards drawn limit).
  -s: use surge (default is fight).
  -t <num>: set the number of threads, default is 4.
  -turnlimit <num>: set the number of turns in a battle, default is 50 (can be used for speedy achievements).
  -v: less verbose output. Omits output about your and enemy's deck and fortress.  
  win:     simulate/optimize for win rate. [default].
  defense: simulate/optimize for win rate + stall rate. can be used for defending deck.
Flags for climb:
  -c: don't try to optimize the commander.
  -L <min> <max>: restrict deck size between <min> and <max>.
  -o: restrict to the owned cards listed in "data/ownedcards.txt".
  -o=<filename>: restrict to the owned cards listed in <filename>.
                       example: -o=data/mycards.txt.
  target <num>: stop as soon as the score reaches <num>.

Operations:
  sim <num>: simulate <num> battles to evaluate a deck.
  climb <num>: perform hill-climbing starting from the given attack deck, using up to <num> battles to evaluate a deck.
  reorder <num>: optimize the order for given attack deck, using up to <num> battles to evaluate an order.
